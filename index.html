Planet Collector!



You wil be given a random number at the start of each game that you will have to match.

There are 4 planets below. By clicking on a planet, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number; but you lose the game if your total score surpasses the random number.

Each planet has a hidden value ranging from 1-12. You can figure each planet's value by clicking on them. Each planet will reset in value if you win/lose the game!

Good Luck!
